Call for Papers

IEEE AFRICON 2026 invites submissions of original, unpublished research papers from researchers, engineers, and practitioners from universities, industry, and research institutions worldwide. The conference theme is "Harnessing Emerging Technologies for Africa’s sustainable development".

Topics of Interest (not limited to)

  • Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning & Deep Learning
  • Computational Intelligence & Evolutionary Computing
  • Cloud Computing, Fog Computing & Edge Computing
  • Big Data Analytics & Data Science
  • 5G/6G Wireless Communications & Networks
  • Antenna Design & Microwave Systems
  • Propagation Models & Channel Estimation
  • Integrated Sensing and Communication (ISAC)
  • Internet of Things (IoT) & Embedded Systems
  • Power Electronics & Drives
  • Renewable Energy Systems & Smart Grids
  • Cybersecurity & Privacy
  • Robotics & Autonomous Systems
  • Biomedical Engineering & Health Informatics
  • Signal Processing, Image & Video Processing

Paper Submission

All papers must be submitted electronically through the EDAS paper management system. Submitted papers must be original work not previously published or currently under review for any other conference or journal.

Papers should be written in English and must comply with the IEEE two-column format. The maximum paper length is 6 pages (including references). Authors may purchase up to 2 additional pages at an extra charge.

Author Guidelines

Paper Format

  • Papers must be formatted using the IEEE two-column conference template.
  • Templates are available for Microsoft Word and LaTeX.
  • Maximum length: 6 pages including all figures, tables, and references.
  • Up to 2 additional pages may be purchased during registration.
  • All submissions must be in PDF format.

Review Process

All papers will undergo rigorous double-blind peer review. Authors must ensure their submissions are anonymized — remove author names, affiliations, and acknowledgements from the submitted manuscript.

Publication

Accepted and presented papers will be submitted for inclusion in the IEEE Xplore Digital Library. Authors of accepted papers must register for the conference and present their work in person (or virtually).
