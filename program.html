Technical Program

The IEEE AFRICON 2026 technical program will feature a rich selection of peer-reviewed paper presentations organized into parallel technical sessions, keynote addresses, invited talks, workshops, and tutorials.

The full technical program will be published after the paper acceptance notifications in August 2026. Check back for updates.

Keynote Speakers

IEEE AFRICON 2026 will feature distinguished keynote speakers from academia, industry, and research institutions. Keynote speaker announcements will be made progressively. Visit the Speakers page for details.

Workshops & Tutorials

Proposals for workshops and tutorials are welcome. Workshops and tutorials provide an opportunity for in-depth exploration of specialized topics relevant to the conference theme.

Workshop and tutorial proposals should be submitted to workshops@ieee-africon2026.org.
