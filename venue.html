Conference Venue

GIMPA
Accra, Ghana

IEEE AFRICON 2026 will be held at GIMPA (Ghana Institute of Management and Public Administration), a premier conference venue in Accra, Ghana, featuring state-of-the-art facilities, multiple conference halls, exhibition spaces, and modern technology infrastructure.

Accommodation

A range of accommodation options are available near GIMPA in Accra, from luxury hotels to budget-friendly guesthouses. Conference delegates will benefit from specially negotiated rates at partner hotels.

Radisson Blu Hotel & Convention Centre

Adjacent to the KCC, the Radisson Blu offers 292 rooms with modern amenities, swimming pool, and fitness centre. Special conference rates available for IEEE AFRICON 2026 delegates.

Marriott Hotel Kigali

Located in the heart of Kigali, the Marriott offers luxury accommodation with panoramic city views. A short drive from the KCC.

Serena Hotel Kigali

Set in lush tropical gardens, the Serena offers an authentic African experience with world-class facilities.

Getting There

By Air

Accra is served by Kotoka International Airport (ACC), which offers direct flights to major African cities and international hubs including Lagos, Johannesburg, Nairobi, London, Amsterdam, and other international destinations.

From the Airport

The airport is approximately 5 km from GIMPA. Taxis and ride-sharing services are readily available. The journey typically takes 15–20 minutes.

About Accra

Accra, the capital and largest city of Ghana, is a vibrant coastal city known for its rich cultural heritage, friendly atmosphere, and rapidly growing technology and innovation ecosystems. As one of West Africa's most developed cities, Accra offers a unique blend of modern facilities and authentic African culture.

Ghana is a leader in technology and innovation on the African continent, hosting major tech companies, startups, and research institutions. The country's commitment to ICT development and its position in West Africa makes Accra a fitting host for the 18th edition of IEEE AFRICON.

Visitors can explore the Cape Coast Castle, the vibrant Makola Market, the Osu Castle, Labadi Beach, and the nearby Kakum National Park, all within reach of Accra.
