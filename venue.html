Conference Venue

GIMPA
Accra, Ghana

IEEE AFRICON 2027 will be held at GIMPA (Ghana Institute of Management and Public Administration), a premier conference venue in Accra, Ghana, featuring state-of-the-art facilities, multiple conference halls, exhibition spaces, and modern technology infrastructure.

Getting There

By Air

Accra is served by Kotoka International Airport (ACC), which offers direct flights to major African cities and international hubs including Lagos, Johannesburg, Nairobi, London, Amsterdam, and other international destinations.

From the Airport

The airport is approximately 5 km from GIMPA. Taxis and ride-sharing services (including Uber and Bolt) are readily available. The journey typically takes 15–20 minutes depending on traffic.

About Accra

Accra, the capital and largest city of Ghana, is a vibrant coastal city known for its rich cultural heritage, friendly atmosphere, and rapidly growing technology and innovation ecosystems. As one of West Africa's most developed cities, Accra offers a unique blend of modern facilities and authentic African culture.

Ghana is a leader in technology and innovation on the African continent, hosting major tech companies, startups, and research institutions. The country's commitment to ICT development and its position in West Africa makes Accra a fitting host for the 19th edition of IEEE AFRICON.

Visitors can explore the Cape Coast Castle, the vibrant Makola Market, the Osu Castle, Labadi Beach, and the nearby Kakum National Park, all within reach of Accra.
