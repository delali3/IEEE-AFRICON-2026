About the Conference

IEEE AFRICON 2026 is the 18th edition of the biennial IEEE AFRICON conference, organized under the auspices of IEEE Region 8 and the IEEE South Africa Section. The conference will be held at GIMPA, Kigali, Rwanda from November 18th to 20th, 2026.

The conference theme is "Innovating Africa's Future Through Technology", reflecting the commitment to fostering world-class research and innovation that drives sustainable development across the African continent and beyond.

IEEE AFRICON 2026 will feature world-class plenary speakers, technical symposiums, industry and academic panels, workshops, tutorials, and invited tracks on emerging technologies. The conference provides a platform for students, researchers, and the industry worldwide to meet, network, and pursue collaboration opportunities.

Thematic Research Areas

  • Artificial and Computational Intelligence
  • Cloud Computing and Data Analytics
  • Wireless Communication Systems
  • Antennas, Microwave Systems and Propagation Models
  • Integrated Sensing & Communication and IoT
  • Power Engineering & Energy Systems
  • Cybersecurity
  • Robotics and Autonomous Systems
  • Signal Processing and Multimedia
  • Biomedical Engineering

Organizers

IEEE AFRICON 2026 is co-sponsored by IEEE Region 8 and the IEEE South Africa Section, and is organized in partnership with the IEEE Rwanda Subsection. The conference will be hosted at GIMPA, the premier conference and exhibition facility in Kigali.
