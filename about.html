About the Conference

IEEE AFRICON 2027 is the 18th edition of the biennial IEEE AFRICON conference, organized under the auspices of IEEE Region 8 and the IEEE South Africa Section. The conference will be held at GIMPA, Accra, Ghana from September 23rd to 25th, 2027.

The conference theme is "Harnessing Emerging Technologies for Africa’s sustainable development", reflecting the commitment to fostering innovation across Africa.

Organizers

IEEE AFRICON 2027 is co-sponsored by IEEE Region 8 and the IEEE South Africa Section, in partnership with the IEEE Ghana Section.
