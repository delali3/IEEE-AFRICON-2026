AFRICON 2026 Program

The 2026 IEEE AFRICON Program will include;

  1. Technical Paper Presentations
  2. Plenary Sessions
  3. Panel Sessions
  4. Tutorials
  5. Workshops
  6. Exhibitions
  7. Tours in and around Kigali and Rwanda

Note to All Attendees

Registration fees include:

A full registration includes access to all parts of the conference: Keynotes, Panels, Workshops, Tutorials, Exhibitions, Welcome Reception, Coffee breaks, lunches, (1) Conference Banquet ticket, and the full technical program and proceedings.

A limited registration includes access to some parts of the conference: Keynotes, Panels, Exhibitions, Welcome Reception, Coffee breaks, lunches, (1) Conference Banquet ticket, and the full Technical program and proceedings.
NB: Does not include Workshops and Tutorials

A tutorials only registration includes access to the workshops, tutorials and exhibitions only plus Coffee breaks and lunches on the days of attendance.

Note to All Authors

  1. All paper presenters shall pay FULL registration fees for the conference.
  2. For authors with multiple accepted papers, one full registration is valid for one (1) paper. One author registration will cover the publication expenses of only One accepted paper. For each additional accepted paper associated to the same registration (up to 3 papers per registration), $50 USD will be charged per additional paper.
  3. Authors presenting more than 3 papers must pay an additional FULL registration for all the papers. For each additional accepted paper associated to the same registration, a $50 USD fee will be charged.
  4. Where two people want to present papers, each person must pay full registration as per number 2 above depending on the number of papers to be presented.
  5. To be published in the IEEE Africon 2026 Conference Proceedings and to be eligible for publication in IEEE Xplore, at least one author of an accepted paper is required to register for the conference at the FULL (member or non-member) rate and present the paper.
  6. Non-IEEE Student Members will pay the Non-Member rate.

Student Authors

In order for a paper to be published, at least one of the authors from each paper must pay the full author registration fee and attend the conference to present the paper.
